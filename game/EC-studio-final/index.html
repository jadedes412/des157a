how to play

There are two players. The player whose turn it is pounces on two random toys. Each toy is worth a certain number of points:

  • broken toy

    = 0 points (player turn OVER!)

  • worm

    = 2 points

  • yarn

    = 3 points

  • duck

    = 4 points

  • mouse

    = 5 points

  • fish

    = 6 points

The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless a broken toy appears. If both toys are broken, that player immediately loses.

After each roll, the player can pounce again or skip their turn. The first player to reach 30 points or higher wins.
